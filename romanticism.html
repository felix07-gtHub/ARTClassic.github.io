Romanticism

1789-1837

The Romanticism period was a cultural and artistic movement that emerged in the late 18th century and reached its peak during the first half of the 19th century. It originated in Europe as a reaction against the Enlightenment's emphasis on reason, order, and logic, as well as the industrialization that was rapidly transforming society. Romanticism celebrated individual expression, emotion, and imagination, often focusing on the sublime and mysterious aspects of nature and human experience.
